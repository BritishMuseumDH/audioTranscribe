How to transcribe our audio files

Transcription of our audio is pretty straightforward.

All we want you to do, is to listen to the audio snippet you are presented with (10 seconds) and try to transcribe what is being said into the text box.

The audio player has very simple controls.

  • Rewind
  • Play and pause
  • Fast forward
  • Mute

The audio will not play until you ask it to do so. Stop it whenever you need to do so and rewind and fast forward as required. Do not worry if you cannot transcribe everything. Each card will be worked on by several people and then these transcriptions will be merged via computing scripts for final curatorial approval.

Thank you for participating.
